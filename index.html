Well-made Image Optimiser

How to use

This tool compresses most image files and converts to the modern standard .AVIF format. Add your images to ensure they match the standards I use internally.

Tips

  • This tool does not intelligently resize images. It is best practice to use images that are 1-2x the maximum size you expect to display them at.
  • If your images will be displayed on a white background, you should pre-convert them to JPEG before uploading (as transparency is not required).
  • Optimised images begin with "x-" or "opt_" in their filename. This makes it easy to see at a glance which of your images have been optimised.
